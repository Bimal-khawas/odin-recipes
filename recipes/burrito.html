Burrito Recipe

Description

These beef burritos are a delicious and satisfying meal that combines seasoned ground beef with fluffy rice, hearty beans, and a variety of fresh toppings, all wrapped in a warm tortilla. The beef is cooked with a blend of spices that give it a rich and savory flavor, while the rice and beans add a comforting texture and heartiness to the dish. Topped with melted cheese, fresh salsa, and a dollop of sour cream, these burritos are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend treat. They are easy to customize with your favorite toppings like guacamole, lettuce, or jalapeños, making them a versatile and crowd-pleasing meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 400 g (14 oz) can refried beans
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup fresh salsa or pico de gallo
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Directions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat if necessary.
  3. Stir in the cumin, smoked paprika, chilli powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to allow the spices to meld with the beef.
  4. Warm the flour tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
  5. To assemble the burritos, spread a spoonful of refried beans onto each tortilla, followed by a layer of cooked rice and a generous portion of the seasoned beef. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
  6. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly from one end to the other to enclose the filling.
  7. Serve the burritos with a dollop of sour cream, fresh salsa, and a sprinkle of fresh coriander on top. Enjoy!
