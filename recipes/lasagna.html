Lasagna Recipe

Description

A classic lasagna is the epitome of comfort cooking — hearty, cheesy, and deeply satisfying. This recipe layers tender pasta sheets with a rich, slow-simmered beef and tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and a generous blanket of melted mozzarella and Parmesan. Every forkful delivers the perfect balance of savoury meat, silky sauce, and gooey cheese.

Perfect for feeding a crowd or making ahead for a stress-free dinner, this lasagna tastes even better the next day after the flavours have had time to meld. Serve it with a crisp green salad and garlic bread for a truly unforgettable meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions/Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute more.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned all over. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg, a pinch of salt, and half the Parmesan.
  5. Preheat your oven to 180 °C (350 °F). Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer pasta sheets over the sauce, followed by more meat sauce, dollops of the ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of mozzarella. Repeat for 3-4 layers, finishing with a top layer of pasta, remaining sauce, and all the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil and serve.
